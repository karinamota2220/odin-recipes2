lasagna

Definitely easy to make and very yummy.

The lasagna noodles I used didn’t require boiling, I do wonder if that made a difference as I added 1/2 cup water along the edges and it turned out great. I put pan on a cookie sheet but it didn’t bubble over. I accidentally put in too much mozzarella but I suspect no one would complain. Used stored bought sauce, tomato basil & garlic. Took the advice of another commenter here, baked covered for 50 instead of 40 minutes before removing the cover to bake again for 10 minutes. Happy with the result. I think this is a great base recipe covering the basics of how to make a lasagna with today’s resources and lifestyles, modifications are easily made to suit individual’s preferences, like which store bought sauce to use, different types of meat in the sauce etc.. overall, I’m happy with this!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Beef
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
